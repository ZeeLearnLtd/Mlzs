Get to Know Us

Mount Litera Zee School is one of the leading and fastest-growing chains of K-12 schools in India. Started under the pioneering leadership of Zee Learn Ltd., Mount Litera Zee School is on a mission to redefine the education landscape in the country. The purpose is to empower India's youth and children through quality education and enable them to realise their unique potential. With exposure to state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities, exceptional educators and innovative pedagogy, Mount Litera Zee School aims to nurture students by providing a robust and child-centric learning environment that prepares them for success in an ever-evolving world.
