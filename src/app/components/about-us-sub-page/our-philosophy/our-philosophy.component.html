Mount Litera Zee School is committed to setting benchmarks in educational excellence, ensuring its legacy evolves with the changing needs of the 21st century.

Mount Litera Zee School aims to provide a holistic and unique educational experience, equipping children with the skills and knowledge needed for a successful life in contemporary society. Our vision is to:

To create an excellent educational institution synthesising the human values with the highest quality of teaching–learning using modern technology-driven tools for preparing a well-rounded personality for our society.
To shape global minds on the Indian soil: To ensure this, the school will give every student an access to world-class infrastructure, and an innovative curriculum that promotes the all-round development of the child imbibing traditional values.
Vision of Zee Learn

"We provide the environment to ignite, nurture, and unleash your potential and talent."

At Mount Litera Zee School, we believe education should transcend traditional boundaries and prepare children for life, not just exams. Through our Unique Learner Profile (ULP), we honour each child’s individual strengths, learning styles, and aspirations, enabling us to teach them in a way that suits their unique learning needs. We create ecosystems of growth where creativity, inquiry, and exploration lead the learning process, empowering students to take ownership of their educational journey. Our approach integrates cutting-edge pedagogy, heutagogy-based learning or self-determined learning, technology-driven tools, and cross-disciplinary methods to foster conceptual clarity, critical thinking, and 10-fold holistic development.

Beyond academics, we nurture life skills, values, and a strong sense of purpose, preparing students to become confident, compassionate, and resilient leaders. With a focus on real-world problem-solving, students are equipped to adapt, thrive, and shape the future with empathy, creativity, and integrity, rooted in Indian values and enriched by global perspectives.
