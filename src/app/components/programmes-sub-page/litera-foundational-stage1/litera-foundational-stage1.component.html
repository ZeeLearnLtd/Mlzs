The Litera Foundational Stage 1 nurtures young minds through an activity-based, experiential approach aligned with the NCF 2023 framework. We focus on building strong literacy and numeracy skills while fostering creativity, confidence, and holistic growth.

Key Features:

A safe, joyful, and inclusive environment that inspires curiosity and a love for learning.

Hands-on, play-based learning to enhance discovery and engagement.

Personalised learning paths that respect individual progress.

AR-based tools and gamified experiences for dynamic education.

Holistic Progress Card for comprehensive growth tracking.

Litera Nova prepares learners by ensuring a smooth transition to advanced learning, promoting intellectual, social, and emotional development, and laying the foundation for lifelong success.

Shaping stars, where sparks evolve into brilliance!

Designed in alignment with NCF 2023, the Litera Foundational Stage 2 fosters curiosity, creativity, and foundational literacy and numeracy skills while supporting cognitive, social, and emotional development.

Key Features:

Core skill development : Strong emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving.

Critical thinking and personalised learning: Encouraging curiosity and adapting to unique learner needs through Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles. The Unique Learner Profile (ULP) ensures personalised and equitable learning opportunities.

10-Dimensional approach: Integrating language, mathematics, and life skills with project-based experiential learning.

Gamified and AR learning: Engaging digital tools that make abstract concepts interactive and fun.

Periodic progressive assessments (PPA): Continuous progress tracking with insights through the Holistic Progress Report.

Real-world connections: Lessons linked to everyday experiences for meaningful learning.

Holistic development: Litera Nova fosters holistic growth by integrating key teaching tools, with programmes like Litera Avishkaar and Freadom app enhancing foundational skills in Stage 2.

Litera Nova ensures mastery of foundational skills while promoting equity, creativity, and critical thinking—empowering children for future academic success.

The Litera Middle Stage at Mount Litera Zee School focuses on fostering intellectual and social growth as students begin to connect with the broader community. We offer a transformative journey that nurtures students' transition to academic excellence, emphasising intellectual, social, and personal growth. Guided by the Litera Nova approach, students experience personalised and adaptive learning tailored to their unique needs, fostering critical thinking and self-directed learning.

The Litera Secondary Stage at Mount Litera Zee School provides students with the opportunity to shape their academic journey based on their individual interests, abilities, and future aspirations. Students are guided to select subjects within the National Curriculum Framework, choosing from streams like Commerce, Science, or Arts, with specialised electives to match their career goals. Students engage in interdisciplinary projects, competency-based assessments, and collaborative learning, which build critical thinking, problem-solving, decision-making, and communication skills.
